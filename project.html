HealthCore Web Platform

AI-powered, HIPAA-compliant patient management serving 500+ hospitals — 40% fewer diagnostic errors and $12M in annual savings.

Client Apollo Health Systems
Year 2023 – 2024
Sprint 18-Month Agile
Industry Healthcare / MedTech
Overview

Revolutionising diagnostics with AI intelligence

Apollo Health Systems faced fragmented patient data across 500+ facilities — overwhelming clinical staff and causing costly diagnostic delays. Revti Digital designed and engineered a unified AI-powered platform that connects every hospital, surfaces real-time insights, and assists physicians with evidence-based recommendations.

Full HIPAA, HL7 FHIR R4, and ISO 27001 compliance from sprint one — zero security incidents since April 2024 launch.

The Challenge

500+ hospitals, 15 different EMR systems, zero unified intelligence — causing diagnostic inconsistencies and dangerous data silos.

💡

Our Approach

Microservices with an AI inference engine, FHIR R4 federation layer, and a clinical-grade React UI with 200+ purpose-built components.

📈

The Impact

40% fewer diagnostic errors, 3× faster reporting, $12M annual savings, 99.99% uptime across all 500+ hospitals.

🛡️

Compliance First

Full HIPAA, HL7 FHIR R4, and ISO 27001 compliance baked in from sprint one — zero security incidents since launch.

Our Process

From discovery to deployment

🔍
Phase 01 · Weeks 1–4

Discovery & Research

80+ stakeholder interviews across 12 hospitals. Mapped 200+ clinical workflows and synthesised 40 pain points into a strategic platform blueprint.

📐
Phase 02 · Weeks 5–10

Architecture & Strategy

Designed a microservices platform with FastAPI backend, TensorFlow AI models, React frontend, AWS GovCloud infrastructure, and FHIR R4 API layer unifying 15 EMR systems.

🎨
Phase 03 · Weeks 8–16

Design & Prototyping

Clinical-grade design system with 200+ components. Six rounds of usability testing with actual clinicians. WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility enforced from first wireframe.

⚙️
Phase 04 · Weeks 12–52

Engineering & AI Development

18-person team across four tracks: AI model training (12 models, 2M+ medical images), backend, frontend, and EMR integrations with Epic, Oracle Health, and Meditech.

🚀
Phase 05 · Month 18

Deployment & Scale

Blue-green rollout across 500+ hospitals with zero downtime. 12,000 clinical staff trained in 90 days. Continuous anomaly detection ensures 99.99% uptime.
